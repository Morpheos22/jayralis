# Responsible disclosure policy:
# If you discover a security vulnerability on jayralis.fyi, please report
# it to Ceo@jayralis.fyi with full details. We commit to acknowledging

# 5 business days. Please do not publicly disclose the issue until we
# have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate.
#
# Scope: https://www.jayralis.fyi and all subdomains.
# Out of scope: denial-of-service, social engineering, physical attacks.
# Rewards: We do not currently offer a monetary bug bounty, but
